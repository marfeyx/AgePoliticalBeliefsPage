why is this happening?

1

Habit

Old people = lifetime voters. Young people = first time ever maybe? Habits stick.

2

Stability

Older folks own homes, have families, roots in communities. They care who's running things.

3

Awareness

Campaigns target people who've voted before. Young people fall off the radar.

4

It's Growing Though

Young voters are rising. 2018 was a bump. Social media engagement = real votes now.
