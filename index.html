why does this even matter?

🗳️

democratic voice

when young people don't vote, politicians focus on older demographics. your voice literally determines policy direction.

📱

climate action

you'll live with climate consequences longer. higher youth turnout = more climate-focused policies. it's that simple.

💰

economic policy

student debt, housing costs, job markets - these are shaped by who votes. your generation's future literally depends on it.

🤝

collective power

100M eligible voters aged 18-29. if you all showed up, you'd dominate every election. imagine that.
